What is roadmap.sh?

roadmap.sh is a community effort to create learning paths, guides, project ideas and other similar content to help developers grow in their carreers.

What are the plans for roadmap.sh?

Our goal is to continue expanding roadmap.sh as the go-to resource for developers at all levels. We're actively working on adding more role-specific roadmaps, interactive learning features, better collaboration tools for teams, and integrations with popular developer tools. Stay tuned — we're just getting started!

How is roadmap.sh built?

roadmap.sh is an open-source project powered by a modern web stack including Node.js, React, and TypeScript. The content is community-driven and maintained through contributions on GitHub. This collaborative approach ensures the roadmaps are always evolving and relevant.

Can I use roadmap.sh in my team?

Absolutely! roadmap.sh is a great resource for onboarding, mentoring, and skill planning. You can share specific roadmaps with your team, or even create custom ones tailored to your internal roles and tech stacks.

How can I create custom roadmaps?

You can create custom roadmaps directly on roadmap.sh using our roadmap builder. Simply sign in, start from scratch or fork an existing roadmap, and use our visual editor to customize it to your needs. It’s perfect for individuals, teams, or communities.

Is roadmap.sh really 7th most starred project on GitHub?

Yes — as of now, roadmap.sh is one of the top 10 most starred repositories on GitHub, currently ranking 7th. We’re incredibly proud of the community support and contributions that helped us reach this milestone.
